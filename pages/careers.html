Careers at Nexabay

Join the team building Africa's Everything Platform

🌍 Why Work at Nexabay?

Nexabay is on a mission to become Africa's most important technology platform — a single place where every Nigerian and African can shop, work, learn, travel, eat, heal and thrive. We are building something that has never existed before at this scale on the continent, and we want passionate, driven people to be part of this journey.

Working at Nexabay means you are building real products that affect real people's lives across Nigeria and beyond. This is not just a job — it is a chance to be part of something historic.

  • 💰 Competitive salary in Nigerian Naira
  • 🏠 Remote-first work culture
  • 📈 Equity/stock options for early team members
  • 🎓 Learning and development budget
  • 🏥 Health insurance coverage
  • 🌍 Opportunity to build products used across Africa
  • ⚡ Fast-moving, high-impact startup environment
  • 🤝 Collaborative and inclusive team culture

💡 Our Values

🎯 Impact First
We measure our success by how much we improve the lives of our users across Nigeria and Africa. Every decision starts with the question — does this help our users?
⚡ Move Fast
We build quickly, ship often and learn from our users. Speed matters in a competitive market and we embrace rapid iteration over perfect planning.
🤝 Trust and Transparency
We are honest with each other, with our users and with our partners. We communicate openly and treat everyone with respect.
🌍 Africa First
We build for Africa. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities of the African market and we build products that work in our context.
📈 Ownership Mentality
Every team member thinks and acts like an owner. We take responsibility for our work, our mistakes and our growth.

💼 Open Positions

We are a growing startup and are always looking for exceptional talent. Here are our current openings:

Senior Frontend Developer

💻 Engineering 🏠 Remote ⏰ Full-time

We are looking for a passionate frontend developer with strong HTML, CSS and JavaScript skills to help us build and improve our web platform. You will work directly on the core Nexabay experience used by thousands of Nigerians.

Requirements: 2+ years experience with HTML/CSS/JS, experience with Firebase or similar backend services, strong eye for mobile-first design, ability to work independently and deliver results.

Nice to have: Experience with React or Vue, knowledge of Nigerian e-commerce market, previous startup experience.

Customer Support Specialist

💬 Support 🏠 Remote ⏰ Full-time

We need a warm, patient and solutions-focused customer support specialist to manage user inquiries, resolve disputes and ensure every Nexabay user has an excellent experience on the platform.

Requirements: Excellent written and spoken English, empathy and patience when dealing with frustrated users, ability to learn new software quickly, availability during support hours (including weekends), experience in customer service role is a plus.

Nice to have: Experience with Nigerian e-commerce or marketplace platforms, knowledge of Hausa, Igbo or Yoruba languages.

Digital Marketing Manager

📣 Marketing 🏠 Remote ⏰ Full-time

We are looking for a creative, data-driven digital marketer to grow Nexabay's presence across social media, build our brand and drive user acquisition across Nigeria. You will own our Instagram, TikTok, Twitter and WhatsApp marketing strategy.

Requirements: 2+ years of digital marketing experience, proven track record of growing social media accounts, strong content creation skills, understanding of Nigerian social media culture and trends, experience with paid advertising on Meta or Google.

Nice to have: Video editing skills for TikTok/Reels content, influencer marketing experience, understanding of Nigerian fintech or e-commerce market.

Business Development Executive

📈 Business 🏠 Remote / Lagos ⏰ Full-time

We need a hustler to help us onboard restaurants, hotels, hospitals, service providers and other businesses onto the Nexabay platform. You will be the face of Nexabay to potential partners and play a key role in growing our supply side across all 12 sections.

Requirements: Strong communication and negotiation skills, self-motivated and target-driven, ability to build relationships quickly, willingness to do outreach calls and meetings, experience in sales or business development is preferred.

Nice to have: Existing network in the Lagos business community, experience in marketplace or platform business models.

UI/UX Designer

🎨 Design 🏠 Remote ⏰ Full-time / Contract

We are looking for a talented designer to improve the visual quality, usability and overall user experience of the Nexabay platform. You will design new features, improve existing flows and ensure Nexabay looks and feels world-class on every device.

Requirements: Strong portfolio of mobile-first web or app designs, proficiency in Figma or similar design tools, understanding of user-centered design principles, ability to translate complex flows into simple, beautiful interfaces.

Nice to have: Experience designing for Nigerian or African users, knowledge of accessibility standards, motion design or animation skills.

🎓 Internship Program

Are you a student or recent graduate passionate about technology, business or marketing in Africa? Nexabay offers a 3-month paid internship program for exceptional young Nigerians who want to learn by doing.

Our interns work on real projects with real impact — not just making coffee. Past interns have worked on product features, marketing campaigns and user research that are now part of the live platform.

We accept internship applications on a rolling basis. To apply, send your CV and a short note about why you want to intern at Nexabay to nexabay7@gmail.com with the subject "Internship Application".
