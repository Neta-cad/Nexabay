At Nexabay, we take the trust of our buyers and sellers seriously. This Refund and Buyer Protection Policy explains your rights as a buyer, the circumstances under which refunds are issued, and how we handle disputes between buyers and sellers on our platform. This policy applies to all purchases made through Nexabay's official checkout system powered by Paystack.

Important: This policy only covers transactions completed through Nexabay's official payment system. Payments made outside the platform — directly to a seller's bank account, via cash, or through any other means — are not covered by this policy and Nexabay assumes no responsibility for such transactions.

1. What is Nexabay Buyer Protection?

Nexabay Buyer Protection is our guarantee to every buyer that their money is safe when shopping on our platform. When you pay through Nexabay, your payment is held securely and only released to the seller after your order is successfully fulfilled. If something goes wrong, we are here to help.

Buyer Protection covers you in the following situations:

  • ✅ You did not receive your order within the stated delivery period
  • ✅ The item you received is significantly different from the listing description
  • ✅ The item arrived damaged or defective through no fault of your own
  • ✅ The seller cancelled your order after you paid
  • ✅ You received a counterfeit or fake item
  • ✅ The service you paid for was not delivered as described

2. Full Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund in the following circumstances:

  • Order Not Received: Your order has not arrived within 5 business days after the seller's stated maximum delivery date, and the seller is unable to provide proof of shipment or delivery.
  • Seller Cancelled: The seller cancelled your order for any reason after payment was made.
  • Counterfeit Item: You received an item that is clearly counterfeit, fake or a replica, when the listing did not disclose this.
  • Item Significantly Not as Described: The item you received is materially different from what was described or shown in the listing — for example, a completely different product, wrong size, wrong colour or missing major features.
  • Damaged on Arrival: The item arrived visibly damaged due to poor packaging or mishandling during delivery, provided you report it within 24 hours of receipt with photographic evidence.

To qualify for a full refund, you must file your dispute within 7 days of the stated delivery date and provide supporting evidence such as photographs, screenshots or any other relevant documentation.

3. Partial Refund and the 50% Guarantee

In cases where the facts of a dispute are unclear, both parties have conflicting accounts, or where partial fault lies on both sides, Nexabay may offer a partial refund as a fair settlement.

Our 50% Buyer Protection Guarantee means that in any genuine dispute where:

  • There is credible evidence of a problem with the order
  • But the full extent of the issue cannot be definitively verified
  • And both parties cannot reach an agreement

Nexabay will, at its discretion, offer the buyer a minimum of 50% of the transaction value as a goodwill settlement. This is not automatic — it requires a formal dispute to be filed and reviewed by our team.

Situations that may result in a partial refund instead of a full refund include:

  • Minor discrepancies between the listing and the item received that do not significantly affect the item's value or usability
  • Delivery delays caused partly by the buyer providing an incorrect address
  • Items that were used or damaged by the buyer before a dispute was filed
  • Disputes filed after the 7-day window without a valid reason for the delay

4. How to File a Dispute

If you have a problem with an order, follow these steps:

  • Step 1 — Contact the Seller First: Before filing a formal dispute, try to resolve the issue directly with the seller through your order dashboard. Most issues can be resolved quickly at this stage.
  • Step 2 — File a Dispute: If the seller does not respond within 24 hours or is unable to resolve the issue, contact Nexabay support at nexabay7@gmail.com or WhatsApp +234 907 651 9852 with the subject "Order Dispute". Include your Order ID, a clear description of the problem, and supporting photos or screenshots.
  • Step 3 — Investigation: Our team will review the case, contact both the buyer and seller, and make a determination within 48 hours. We may request additional evidence from either party.
  • Step 4 — Resolution: Once a decision is made, we will notify both parties. Approved refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.

Important: All disputes must be filed within 7 days of the stated or expected delivery date. Disputes filed after this period will only be considered in exceptional circumstances at Nexabay's sole discretion.

5. Situations NOT Covered by Buyer Protection

The following situations are not covered by Nexabay's Buyer Protection Policy:

  • Transactions completed outside Nexabay's official payment system
  • Digital goods or services that have already been accessed, downloaded or used
  • Items that were accurately described in the listing but the buyer changed their mind
  • Disputes filed more than 7 days after the expected delivery date without valid justification
  • Damage caused by the buyer after receipt of the item
  • Items lost due to the buyer providing an incorrect delivery address
  • Disputes involving accounts that have been suspended for policy violations
  • Transactions involving prohibited items that should not have been listed on the platform
  • Disputes where the buyer has already confirmed receipt of the order in the system

6. Refund Processing

Timeline: Once a refund is approved by our team, it will be processed within 3-5 business days. The refund will be returned to the original payment method used for the transaction.

Card Refunds: Refunds to debit or credit cards may take an additional 3-7 business days to appear in your account, depending on your bank. This is outside Nexabay's control and is a standard bank processing time.

Bank Transfer Refunds: If your original payment was made via bank transfer, your refund will be sent to the bank account you provide during the dispute process. This typically takes 1-2 business days after approval.

Partial Refunds: Partial refund amounts are calculated based on the transaction value at the time of purchase, not including any delivery fees, unless the delivery issue is the basis of the dispute.

7. Seller Protection

Nexabay also protects honest sellers from fraudulent buyer claims. We investigate all disputes thoroughly and do not automatically side with the buyer. Sellers who can provide evidence of fulfillment — such as delivery confirmation, tracking information, or proof of shipment — will be protected against false claims.

Buyers found to have filed false or exaggerated dispute claims may have their accounts suspended and may be required to compensate the seller for any losses suffered as a result of the fraudulent claim.

8. Service-Specific Refund Rules

In addition to the general policy above, the following service-specific rules apply:

  • NexaLearn (Courses): Refunds are available within 24 hours of enrollment if you have not accessed more than 10% of the course content. Once significant course content has been accessed, refunds are not available.
  • NexaEvents (Tickets): Event tickets are generally non-refundable unless the event is cancelled by the organizer. If an event is cancelled, a full refund will be issued automatically.
  • NexaTravel (Hotels and Tours): Refund eligibility depends on the cancellation policy stated in the listing. Non-refundable bookings will not be refunded except in cases of verifiable emergency.
  • NexaHealth (Appointments): Appointment fees are refundable if cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellations within 24 hours of the appointment may only receive a 50% refund.
  • NexaRide (Rides and Delivery): Completed rides and deliveries are non-refundable unless the driver failed to show up after payment or the service was materially different from what was booked.
  • NexaFood (Food Orders): Refunds are available if the restaurant cancels your order, the food does not arrive, or the order is significantly wrong. Once food has been collected or delivered and the issue is not reported within 1 hour, refunds are not available.
