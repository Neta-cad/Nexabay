📦 Orders & Delivery

How do I track my order? ▼
After placing an order, go to your Account Dashboard and tap "My Orders". You'll see the current status of your order — Confirmed, Processing, Shipped or Delivered. The seller will update the status as your order progresses. You can also contact the seller directly through the order page.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Delivery times vary by seller and location. Each product listing shows the seller's estimated delivery time. Within Lagos, most sellers deliver within 1-3 business days. For other states, delivery typically takes 3-7 business days. Always check the seller's stated delivery time before ordering.
My order hasn't arrived — what do I do? ▼
First, check your order status in your dashboard. If the status shows "Shipped", contact the seller directly to get the tracking details. If you still haven't received your order after the stated delivery period, you can file a dispute through the order page or contact us on WhatsApp at +234 907 651 9852. Our Buyer Protection covers orders that don't arrive within the stated delivery period.
I received the wrong item — what do I do? ▼
If you receive an item that is significantly different from what was described in the listing, contact the seller immediately through your order dashboard. If the seller does not resolve the issue within 48 hours, contact our support team at nexabay7@gmail.com with photos of the item received. You may be eligible for a full refund under our Buyer Protection Policy.
Can I cancel an order? ▼
You can request an order cancellation before the seller has marked the order as "Shipped". Contact the seller through your order dashboard to request cancellation. Once an order is shipped, cancellation is no longer possible and you would need to follow the return/refund process after receiving the item.

💳 Payments

What payment methods are accepted? ▼
Nexabay accepts all major payment methods through Paystack, including debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfer, USSD and QR code. All payments are processed securely by Paystack, a CBN-licensed payment processor.
My payment failed — what do I do? ▼
If your payment fails, first check that your card has sufficient funds and is enabled for online payments. Some banks require you to activate online/e-commerce transactions through your mobile app or by calling your bank. If the problem persists, try a different card or use bank transfer. Contact us at nexabay7@gmail.com if you were charged but the order was not confirmed.
Is it safe to pay on Nexabay? ▼
Yes, absolutely. All payments on Nexabay are processed by Paystack, which is PCI-DSS compliant and licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria. We never store your card details. Your payment is held in escrow until you confirm receipt of your order, protecting you as a buyer.
I was charged but my order wasn't confirmed — what happens? ▼
This can occasionally happen due to internet connectivity issues. Contact us immediately at nexabay7@gmail.com or WhatsApp +234 907 651 9852 with your payment reference number. We will investigate and either confirm your order or arrange a full refund within 24 hours.

🛍️ Selling on Nexabay

How do I start selling on Nexabay? ▼
Starting to sell on Nexabay is completely free. Simply create an account, go to your Account Dashboard and tap "Seller Dashboard". From there, click "Add New Product" to create your first listing. Your listing will be reviewed by our admin team and published within 24 hours of approval.
How much does Nexabay charge sellers? ▼
Listing products on Nexabay is completely free. We only charge a 5% commission when you make a successful sale. There are no monthly fees, no subscription costs and no hidden charges. You only pay when you earn.
Why was my listing rejected? ▼
Listings may be rejected if they violate our Terms of Service — for example, if the product is prohibited, the description is inaccurate, the images are not genuine, or the listing appears to be fraudulent. You will receive a notification with the reason for rejection. You can edit and resubmit your listing after addressing the issue.
How do I receive payments as a seller? ▼
When a buyer pays for your product, the money is held in your Nexabay wallet. Once the order is confirmed as delivered, the funds (minus Nexabay's commission) become available in your balance. You can then request a withdrawal to your bank account from the Earnings tab in your Seller Dashboard. Withdrawals are processed within 1-2 business days.
Can I sell services, not just products? ▼
Yes! Nexabay supports 12 different service categories. From your Account Dashboard, you can become a Service Provider, Rental Owner, Instructor, Employer, Restaurant Partner, Event Organizer, Travel Agent, Health Provider or Driver — all from the same account, with no additional registration required.

💰 Refunds & Disputes

How do I request a refund? ▼
To request a refund, go to your Account Dashboard, find the order in "My Orders" and tap "Report Issue". Describe the problem and submit your request. Our team will review the case within 48 hours. For faster resolution, you can also contact us directly on WhatsApp at +234 907 651 9852.
How long does a refund take? ▼
Once a refund is approved, it is processed within 3-5 business days. The refund will be returned to the original payment method used. Card refunds may take an additional 3-7 business days to reflect depending on your bank.
What is Nexabay's Buyer Protection? ▼
Nexabay's Buyer Protection covers all purchases made through our official checkout. If your order doesn't arrive, if the item is significantly different from the listing, or if there is a genuine dispute, you may be eligible for a full or partial refund. Disputes must be filed within 7 days of the expected delivery date. Our 50% protection guarantee means that in genuine disputes where fault cannot be clearly determined, we will offer at least 50% back to the buyer.

👤 Account

How do I reset my password? ▼
On the login page, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link in your email within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it. Click the link and follow the instructions to set a new password.
How do I update my phone number or email? ▼
Go to your Account Dashboard and tap "Profile". From there you can update your personal information including your phone number, state and display name. To change your email address, contact us at nexabay7@gmail.com as this requires identity verification.
How do I delete my account? ▼
To delete your Nexabay account, email us at nexabay7@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" from your registered email address. We will process your request within 7 business days. Note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Any pending orders must be resolved before deletion.
My account was suspended — what do I do? ▼
If your account has been suspended, you will have received an email explaining the reason. Contact us at nexabay7@gmail.com to appeal the suspension. Please provide your registered email and a detailed explanation. Our team will review your case within 3 business days. Note that accounts suspended for fraud or severe policy violations may not be eligible for reinstatement.

💸 Withdrawals

How do I withdraw my earnings? ▼
Go to your provider dashboard (Seller, Services, etc.), tap the "Earnings" tab and fill in your bank details in the withdrawal form. Enter the amount you want to withdraw (minimum ₦1,000) and submit your request. Our team will process and transfer the funds to your bank account within 1-2 business days.
How long does a withdrawal take? ▼
Withdrawal requests are reviewed and processed by our finance team within 1-2 business days (Monday to Friday). Requests submitted on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day. You will receive a WhatsApp or email notification when your transfer has been made.
What is the minimum withdrawal amount? ▼
The minimum withdrawal amount on Nexabay is ₦1,000. There is currently no maximum limit on withdrawals, though large withdrawals may be subject to additional verification for security purposes.
My withdrawal hasn't arrived — what do I do? ▼
If your withdrawal hasn't arrived within 2 business days of your request, contact us on WhatsApp at +234 907 651 9852 or email nexabay7@gmail.com with your withdrawal request details. Please include the amount, bank name and account number so we can trace the transfer quickly.
