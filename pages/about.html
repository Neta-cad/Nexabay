🎯 Our Mission

Nexabay was built with one mission — to give every African access to a world-class platform where they can shop, earn, learn, find work and manage their daily needs, all in one place.

We believe every Nigerian deserves the tools to build wealth, grow businesses and improve their lives through technology. No more switching between 10 different apps. Everything is here.

🚀 What We Offer

  • 🛍️ NexaShop — Buy and sell products across Nigeria
  • 🛠️ NexaServices — Hire skilled professionals for any job
  • 🏠 NexaRent — Find houses, apartments and rental items
  • 🎓 NexaLearn — Learn new skills from Nigerian instructors
  • 💼 NexaJobs — Find your next job or hire talent
  • 🍔 NexaFood — Order food from local restaurants
  • 🏥 NexaHealth — Book doctors, pharmacies and labs
  • 🎉 NexaEvents — Buy tickets for events across Nigeria
  • ✈️ NexaTravel — Book hotels, flights and tour packages
  • 🚗 NexaRide — Book rides and delivery services
  • 💳 NexaPay — Secure payments and wallet system
  • 👥 NexaCommunity — Connect with Nigerians nationwide

💪 Why Choose Nexabay

  • ✅ One account for everything — buy, sell, learn, work
  • ✅ Secure payments powered by Paystack escrow
  • ✅ All sellers and providers verified by our admin team
  • ✅ Built specifically for the Nigerian market
  • ✅ Works perfectly on any smartphone
  • ✅ 50% buyer protection guarantee on disputes
