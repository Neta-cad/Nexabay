About Nexabay

Africa's Everything Platform — Built for Africans, by Africans

🎯

Our Mission

Nexabay was built with one mission: to give every African access to a world-class platform where they can buy, sell, rent, learn, find work and manage their finances — all in one place.

We believe every Nigerian and African deserves the tools to build wealth, grow businesses and improve their lives through technology.

📖

Our Story

Nexabay was founded by a team of young Nigerian entrepreneurs who were frustrated with the limitations of existing platforms. We wanted one place that could handle every aspect of daily life — not 10 different apps.

Starting with the belief that Africa deserves better, we built Nexabay to be the most comprehensive platform on the continent — combining shopping, services, education, jobs, food delivery, health, travel, payments and community into one seamless experience.
