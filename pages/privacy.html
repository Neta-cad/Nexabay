Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to Nexabay ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit our platform at nexabay.com or any related mobile or web application (collectively, the "Platform"). Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Platform immediately.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide: When you register for an account, we collect your first and last name, email address, phone number, state of residence and a password. When you set up a seller or provider profile, we may additionally collect your business name, bank account details for withdrawal purposes and identity verification documents where required by Nigerian law.

Transaction Information: When you make a purchase or receive payment on the platform, we collect transaction details including order ID, amount, payment reference number, delivery address and the items purchased. We do not store your debit or credit card details — all card transactions are processed directly and securely by Paystack, our licensed payment processor.

Usage Information: We automatically collect certain information when you use our Platform, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, the pages you visit, the search terms you use, products you view and the time and duration of your sessions. This helps us understand how our Platform is being used and improve it accordingly.

Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access Nexabay, including device model, operating system version and unique device identifiers.

Communications: When you contact our support team, report a dispute or leave a review, we collect and store the content of those communications along with your contact information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create, maintain and secure your Nexabay account, verify your identity and process account-related requests such as password resets.
  • Transaction Processing: To facilitate purchases, bookings, job applications, course enrollments and all other transactions on the Platform, including connecting buyers with sellers and service providers.
  • Payment Processing: To process payments through Paystack, manage withdrawal requests and maintain accurate financial records for all parties.
  • Communication: To send you order confirmations, booking updates, account notifications, security alerts and responses to your support requests. We may also send you promotional emails about new features or offers, which you can opt out of at any time.
  • Platform Improvement: To analyse usage patterns, identify bugs, test new features, conduct research and continuously improve the quality and performance of our Platform.
  • Safety and Security: To detect, investigate and prevent fraudulent transactions, abuse, policy violations and other harmful or illegal activities on the Platform.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable Nigerian laws and regulations, respond to lawful requests from government authorities and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Dispute Resolution: To investigate and resolve disputes between buyers and sellers under our Buyer Protection Policy.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Sellers and Service Providers: When you place an order or make a booking, we share your name, phone number, delivery address and order details with the relevant seller or provider so they can fulfill your request.
  • With Buyers: When a seller receives an order, the buyer's name, phone number and delivery address are shared with the seller for fulfillment purposes only.
  • With Payment Processors: We share necessary transaction information with Paystack to process payments securely. Paystack operates under its own privacy policy and is licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria.
  • With Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party companies that help us operate the Platform, such as cloud hosting (Google Firebase), image hosting (ImgBB) and email services. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property or safety of Nexabay, our users or the public.
  • Business Transfers: If Nexabay is acquired by or merged with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your personal information is stored on Google Firebase servers, which provide enterprise-grade security including data encryption at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols. Your password is stored in an encrypted format and is never visible to any Nexabay staff member.

Payment data is handled entirely by Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria. We never see, access or store your card details at any point during the payment process.

While we implement strong security measures, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information but we continuously work to protect it using industry best practices.

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Nexabay uses browser localStorage and session storage to keep you logged in between visits, remember your shopping cart contents and store your preferences. These are small pieces of data stored directly on your device and are not transmitted to third parties.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. You can clear your browser's local storage at any time through your browser settings, which will log you out of the Platform and clear your cart.

6. Children's Privacy

Nexabay is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@nexabay.com.

7. Your Privacy Rights

As a Nexabay user, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of all personal information we hold about you at any time.
  • Right to Correction: You can update or correct your personal information at any time through your Account Settings page.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us at privacy@nexabay.com. Note that some data may be retained for legal or financial compliance purposes.
  • Right to Opt Out: You can opt out of promotional emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing email we send you.
  • Right to Portability: You can request an export of your personal data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@nexabay.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 14 business days.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Platform may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by Nexabay. This Privacy Policy applies only to our Platform. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the privacy practices of any third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make significant changes, we will notify registered users via email and display a prominent notice on the Platform. The date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated. Your continued use of the Platform after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
